LeadClaw case study

LeadClaw: an AI receptionist and lead capture product for local service businesses.

LeadClaw is a BrightFoundry product built for clinics, trades and service businesses that want their website to capture enquiries, organise leads and support faster follow-up.

Try LeadClaw Ask about a custom AI receptionist

Build snapshot

  • Website assistant / AI receptionist
  • Lead capture and enquiry organisation
  • SaaS onboarding, portal and billing flow
  • Lead intelligence / scraper pipeline for safe preparation

Built as a separate product, not merged into the BrightFoundry website.

The problem

Many local service businesses get website traffic but still lose good enquiries because forms are passive, questions arrive incomplete, and follow-up depends on someone checking email at the right moment.

Enquiries arrive messy

Customers often leave out the details a business needs to quote, book or respond properly.

Follow-up is manual

Small teams need reminders and structure without adding another heavy system to manage.

Lead data gets scattered

Website forms, notes, emails and spreadsheets make it harder to see what is worth actioning.

What I built

A SaaS-style product that combines an AI receptionist, lead capture, enquiry tracking, onboarding, billing and a safer lead intelligence workflow.

AI receptionist

A website assistant that helps collect useful enquiry details before a business responds.

Lead workspace

A place to organise captured enquiries, track status and keep follow-up work visible.

Product infrastructure

Onboarding, portal access, billing state, email flow, analytics and monitoring around the core product.

Key features

Focused around capturing better enquiries and helping small teams respond with less admin.

  • AI receptionist / website assistant for request capture
  • Lead capture and enquiry organisation
  • Lead tracking for workspace visibility
  • Follow-up automation for reminders and next steps
  • SaaS onboarding and portal access
  • Billing and subscription flow
  • Lead intelligence / scraper pipeline for collecting, enriching, scoring, and preparing leads safely

Audience

Clinics, trades and local service businesses.

Positioning

AI receptionist plus lead capture, not a generic chatbot.

BrightFoundry role

Product strategy, build, integration and launch setup.

Tech and build credibility

A real product build with billing, database, email, analytics and monitoring concerns handled carefully.

Product app

Next.js application with SaaS onboarding, account state and a user portal.

Data and billing

Supabase database/auth and Stripe billing for subscription lifecycle management.

Operations

Vercel deployment, Resend email, PostHog analytics, Sentry monitoring and a lead intelligence / scraper pipeline.

Why it matters for local businesses

A good local website should do more than look tidy. It should help a business understand who wants help, what they need, and what to do next. LeadClaw is the product version of that idea.
