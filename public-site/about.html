About BrightFoundry

A small studio for people who want a human behind their website.

BrightFoundry is a one-to-one web studio based in South Wales, combining modern design, practical development and useful AI tools — with the kind of support you’d expect from a person, not a faceless agency.

Who’s behind all this?

A real person, not a giant team of “account managers”.

Hi — I’m Kristian. BrightFoundry is a solo studio, so you work directly with the person designing, building and refining your site or app.

I’ve been coding on and off for years and I’m currently studying Computing & IT with the Open University as ongoing professional development — to keep sharpening my skills and stay up to date with modern best practice.

BrightFoundry is built around a simple idea: you shouldn’t have to chase a web project. You’ll get calm communication, realistic expectations and a clear process — plus a private client area where you can see updates, files and next steps in one place.

Privacy matters here. You can keep things email-only if you prefer, and you’ll never be chased or added to mailing lists. If you become a client, most communication can stay inside your private portal.

I take on a focused mix of work: clean business websites, small web apps and tools, client portals, branding support, and practical AI features where they genuinely help.

Professional standards

Clear communication, good processes and responsible data handling.

I hold a certification in GDPR compliance, meaning every project is designed with responsible data handling, privacy awareness and UK best practices in mind. Whether it’s a simple contact form or a custom login system, your users’ information is treated with care.

I’m also constantly improving through study and real-world building — so the work you get is modern, functional and built on solid foundations.

What BrightFoundry stands for

A mix of calm, clean design and practical, real-world thinking.

Clarity over chaos

Your site or app should feel simple and easy to use — for you and your customers.

One-to-one communication

You talk to the person doing the work. No hand-offs or departments.

Step-by-step improvements

You don’t need everything at once. Start small, then evolve.

Honest expectations

AI as an assistant

Support after launch

What it’s like to work together

A simple, guided process from idea to launch.

1. Friendly chat

We explore your goals and challenges.

2. Clear plan

You get a written outline of scope, time and cost.

3. Design & build

Small steps, regular check-ins and iteration.

4. Launch

We push the project live and ensure it runs smoothly.

5. Support

You can request changes and support through the portal.

6. Partnership

If it works well, we keep improving your system long-term.

Who BrightFoundry is a good fit for

And who it probably isn’t.

A good fit if…

  • You’re a small business, solo founder or local service
  • You value honest communication
  • You want a clean, reliable, modern website
  • You’re open to using modern tools, including AI where useful

Not a good fit if…

  • You need enterprise-scale systems
  • You expect 24/7 instant support
  • You want a huge project delivered unrealistically fast

Not sure?
