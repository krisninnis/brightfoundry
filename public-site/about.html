About BrightFoundry

A small studio for people who want a human behind their website.

BrightFoundry is a one-to-one web studio based in South Wales, combining modern design, practical development and new AI tools — with the kind of support you’d expect from a friend, not a faceless agency.

Who’s behind all this?

A real person, not a giant team of “account managers”.

BrightFoundry is run by a solo designer–developer who’s actively studying Computing & IT with the Open University, while building real projects for real clients.

That means you’re not dealing with a detached agency — you’re working directly with the person designing, building and refining your site or app. You’ll get honest communication, realistic expectations, and someone who actually cares whether this project helps you move forward.

I’m happy to take on a mix of projects: local business sites, small web apps, portals, brand kits, content and experiments with AI — especially if you’re open to trying new ideas and improving things over time.

Professional Standards

Clear communication, good processes and responsible data handling.

I hold a certification in GDPR compliance, meaning every project is designed with responsible data handling, privacy awareness, and UK best practices in mind. Whether it’s a simple contact form or a custom login system, your users’ information is treated with the highest level of care.

I’m also constantly improving my skills through study and real-world projects, ensuring the work you get is modern, functional and built on solid foundations.
