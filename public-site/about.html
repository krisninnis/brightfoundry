About Claw Labs

A small UK product studio for useful websites, AI tools and software systems.

Claw Labs is a hands-on UK product studio based in South Wales, building AI tools, websites, automations and SaaS products with calm, practical delivery.

What makes it different

  • One-to-one build — you work directly with the person doing the work
  • Clear launch plan, written scope and realistic timelines
  • Practical support included, not an optional extra
  • No agency hand-offs, account managers or disappearing acts
  • Private client portal keeps every project organised from day one

Who’s behind all this?

A real person, not a giant team of “account managers”.

Hi ? I?m Kristian. Claw Labs is a solo product studio, so you work directly with the person shaping, designing, building and refining your website, app or automation.

I’ve been coding on and off for years and I’m currently studying Computing & IT with the Open University as ongoing professional development — to keep sharpening my skills and stay up to date with modern best practice.

Claw Labs is built around a simple idea: you shouldn’t have to chase a web project. You’ll get calm communication, realistic expectations and a clear process — plus a private client area where you can see updates, files and next steps in one place.

Privacy matters here. You can keep things email-only if you prefer, and you’ll never be chased or added to mailing lists. If you become a client, most communication can stay inside your private portal.

I take on a focused mix of work: clean business websites, AI receptionists, lead-capture systems, small SaaS products, client portals and practical automations where they genuinely help.

Professional standards

Clear communication, good processes and responsible data handling.

I hold a certification in GDPR compliance, meaning every project is designed with responsible data handling, privacy awareness and UK best practices in mind. Whether it’s a simple contact form or a custom login system, your users’ information is treated with care.

I’m also constantly improving through study and real-world building — so the work you get is modern, functional and built on solid foundations.
