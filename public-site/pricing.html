Website design packages

Built for where you are now, with room to grow later.

Starter Website

Perfect for new businesses, solo founders and simple brochure sites.

  • Up to ~4–5 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact, etc.)
  • Clean, modern, mobile-friendly layout
  • Basic on-page SEO
  • Contact form
  • Private client area for updates + files

Typical range: £900 – £1,200

Business Website

For growing businesses that need more pages, structure and content.

  • ~6–12 pages with custom sections
  • Better content structure & CTAs
  • Blog or resources section (optional)
  • Integrated forms & basic automations
  • Private client area for progress + support

Typical range: £1,500 – £2,500

E-commerce Website

For selling products online with a clean storefront.

  • Product catalogue & categories
  • Basket & checkout flow
  • Payments via Stripe/PayPal (platform-dependent)
  • Basic order notifications
  • Private client area for launch + ongoing support

Typical range: £2,500 – £6,000+

Domain, hosting and premium tools are usually billed separately — we’ll go through options together.
