How support works in practice

You’ll have a simple, clear way to ask for help and see what’s happening.

1. Client portal access

You get a login to the BrightFoundry portal with your projects, files and invoices.

Curious what that looks like? See the client portal in detail →

2. Support requests

Log questions or change requests as support tickets with clear priority levels.

3. Status & updates

See whether your request is open, in progress, waiting on you or completed.

4. Monthly time allowance

Each plan includes a certain amount of “tweak time” for small changes.

5. Extra work

Bigger changes are quoted clearly so you can decide what to prioritise.

6. No long contracts

Care plans can usually be paused or cancelled with notice if things change.

Want someone to “just look after it” for you?

Tell me what you’re running right now (website, app, hosting setup) and how often things change, and I’ll recommend a care plan that makes sense — or tell you honestly if you don’t need one yet.
