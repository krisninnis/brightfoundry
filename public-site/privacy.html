Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

This Privacy Policy explains how BrightFoundry collects, uses, stores and protects your personal data.

1. Who we are

BrightFoundry is a web design and development studio based in the UK. We provide websites, web applications, AI tools, branding and digital services to clients.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address and contact details
  • Business information you choose to share
  • Project requirements, content and files you send
  • Payment and invoicing details, processed securely through third-party providers
  • Website usage data, such as analytics, cookies and browsing behaviour where enabled

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Communicate with you about your enquiry or project
  • Prepare proposals, quotes and work agreements
  • Provide design, development and support services
  • Send invoices and manage payments
  • Improve our website and services

4. Legal basis for processing

We process data under:

  • Contract: when delivering a service you have requested
  • Legitimate interests: basic analytics, communication and portfolio updates
  • Consent: when you opt in to newsletters or optional updates

5. How we store and protect your data

We use secure systems, strong passwords and reputable third-party tools. Your data is only accessed when needed and is never sold.

6. Third-party services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics, such as Google Analytics or privacy-friendly alternatives
  • Email, such as Gmail or Outlook
  • Payments, such as Stripe
  • Hosting, such as Vercel or Netlify

These providers may process data under their own privacy policies.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics and basic functionality. See our Cookies Policy for more details.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. Contact details

If you have questions about this policy or want to make a data request, contact:

Email: brightfoundry.contact@gmail.com
