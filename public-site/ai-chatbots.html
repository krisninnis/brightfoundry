How we build your AI assistant

A simple, honest process — no hype, just useful automation.

1. Collect your content

We gather your website copy, FAQs, documents and any specific answers you want the bot to know.

2. Design the conversation

We decide what the bot should focus on: answering questions, capturing leads, booking calls, or all three.

3. Train & test

We set up the AI, train it on your content and test it with real questions to keep answers accurate and safe.

4. Add to your site or portal

The chatbot can appear as a bubble on your website, inside your client portal or on a dedicated page.

Want to see how the portal works? View the Claw Labs client portal →

5. Monitor & refine

We review what people are asking, improve responses and tweak the flows over time.

6. Optional automations

Connect to email, forms or simple workflows so important messages go straight to the right place.
