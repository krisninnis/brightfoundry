Welcome to Harbour & Hearth

A short story builds trust — especially for local places.

Harbour & Hearth is a small, independent café built around good coffee and simple food done well. We keep things friendly, calm and local — the kind of place you can pop into for five minutes or stay for an hour.

Our menu is seasonal, our bakes change often, and we’re always happy to recommend something if you’re not sure. If you’re nearby, come say hello — we’ll put the kettle on.
